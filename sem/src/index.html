Game Flow

Players take turns. In each turn, a player places one card and draws one from the draw piles.

Objective

Players aim to score as many points as possible. When a player reaches 20 points, the final round is played and the game ends.

Placing Cards

Cards can only be placed by overlapping open corners.

Golden cards can only be placed if the player has the required number of symbols.
